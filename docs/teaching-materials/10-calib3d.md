# 3D Vision Fundamentals

Guide to camera calibration, stereo vision, 3D reconstruction, and Structure from Motion.

[Download PDF]({{ site.baseurl }}/teaching_materials/10-3d-vision.pdf){: .btn .btn-primary .fs-5 .mb-4 .mb-md-0 }

---

## Topics Covered

- **Camera Calibration** - Intrinsics, distortion, undistortion
- **Pose Estimation** - 3D pose with PnP algorithm
- **Stereo Vision** - Rectification, disparity maps
- **3D Reconstruction** - Depth estimation, point clouds
- **Structure from Motion** - Multi-view 3D reconstruction
