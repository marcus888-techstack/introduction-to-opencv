# Multi-Camera Tracking with Re-ID

Guide to MOT, MCMOT, person detection, and re-identification.

## Topics Covered

- **Tracking API** - OpenCV single-object trackers
- **Person Detection** - YOLOv4-tiny detection
- **Person Re-ID** - Deep feature extraction for matching
- **MOT** - Multi-object tracking with SORT concepts
- **MCMOT** - Cross-camera tracking and association
